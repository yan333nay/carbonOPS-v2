# daily-checklist

Generate the daily social media operations checklist to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

## Daily Checklist Template

```markdown
# Daily Social Media Checklist — [DATE]
Generated by: Rhythm (Scheduler) at [HH:MM]

---
## MORNING — Before 9am

### Content Review
- [ ] Check all posts scheduled for today
- [ ] Confirm media assets are ready for each post
- [ ] Review captions for typos or last-minute changes
- [ ] Confirm hashtag sets are attached

### Today's Publishing Schedule
| Time | Platform | Format | Topic | Status |
|------|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| [HH:MM] | [platform] | [format] | [topic] | ⏳ Pending |

---
## MIDDAY — 12pm–2pm

### Engagement
- [ ] Reply to comments on yesterday's posts (all platforms)
- [ ] Like and respond to mentions/tags
- [ ] Check DMs for pending conversations
- [ ] Engage with 5-10 posts in target hashtags

### Monitor
- [ ] Check if morning post is live and performing
- [ ] Note initial engagement rate for morning post

---
## END OF DAY — After 6pm

### Publish Confirmation
- [ ] Confirm all scheduled posts went live
- [ ] Screenshot metrics from each post (save, reach, engagement)

### Tomorrow Prep
- [ ] Verify tomorrow's content is ready and approved
- [ ] Check if any trending topic requires last-minute content change

---
## ALERTS
[Auto-filled by scheduler with any issues detected]

---
## NOTES
[Space for daily observations]
```
