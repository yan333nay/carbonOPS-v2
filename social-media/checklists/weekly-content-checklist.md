# Weekly Content Checklist

Run every Monday morning to plan and track the week's content production. Use `*weekly-checklist` command.

---

## MONDAY — Strategy & Planning

### Last Week Review
- [ ] Read performance report (performance-analyst *generate-report)
- [ ] Identify top 3 posts — what patterns do they share?
- [ ] Identify 3 underperforming posts — diagnose root cause
- [ ] Note audience feedback from comments and DMs
- [ ] Share insights with content-strategist for weekly-review

- [ ] Pull this week's slots from editorial calendar
- [ ] Count: __ Reels | __ Carousels | __ Statics | __ Stories

---

## TUESDAY — Copy Production

- [ ] Hooks written for all posts requiring copy (copy-writer *create-hook)
- [ ] Captions written for all posts (copy-writer *write-caption)
- [ ] Hashtag sets generated per post (copy-writer *generate-hashtags)
- [ ] CTAs assigned to all posts (copy-writer *create-cta)
- [ ] Copy approved by brand manager / content-strategist

---

## WEDNESDAY — Visual & Video Production

- [ ] Video briefs sent to editor for all Reels (video-specialist *create-video-brief)
- [ ] Editing scripts created for all videos (video-specialist *create-editing-script)
- [ ] Audio suggestions provided for each video (video-specialist *suggest-trending-audio)
- [ ] Carousels designed (reference: carousel template)
- [ ] Static posts created
- [ ] All videos edited and submitted for review

---

## THURSDAY — Review & Scheduling

### Content Review
- [ ] Video content reviewed (video-specialist *review-video-content)
- [ ] All captions reviewed for brand voice compliance
- [ ] All visuals checked for brand consistency
- [ ] Final approval on all content

### Scheduling
- [ ] All approved posts scheduled with optimal times (scheduler *schedule-post)
- [ ] Posting schedule shared with team
- [ ] Run scheduler *generate-content-calendar

---

## FRIDAY — Buffer & Next Week Prep

- [ ] 3-day buffer confirmed (Mon/Tue/Wed of next week ready)
- [ ] Any trending opportunity for weekend identified
- [ ] Special dates in next 2 weeks reviewed
- [ ] Next week's trend scan initiated (trend-analyst)
